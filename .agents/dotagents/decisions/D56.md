Date: 2026-07-21

## Decision

Split what `.agents/` held in this repo by audience:

| Content | Home | Tracked? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `dotagents/DECISIONS.md`, `dotagents/decisions/`, `dotagents/NOTES.md` | stays in the repo | **yes** — public |
| `plans/` (11 files incl. `completed/`) | `~/.agents/projects/dotagents/plans/` | no |
| `.agents/AGENTS.md` (repo working notes) | `~/.agents/projects/dotagents/AGENTS.md` | no |

`.gitignore` now excludes `.agents/plans/`, `.agents/AGENTS.md`, and
`.agents/archive/` while leaving `.agents/dotagents/` tracked.

## Why the design log stays public

`CHANGELOG.md` cites **D39, D40, D41, D42, D43** as the rationale for shipped
behavior — literal "See D43" / "See D41" lines in released notes. Moving
`decisions/` private would turn five public citations into references no reader
can resolve. `README.md` also advertises the log as "the 'why' behind every
rule", which is part of what makes this repo useful as a reference.

## Why the plans move

Plans are private working material that happened to sit in a public repo, and
the friction was real: `consolidate_agents_private_sync.md` had to be scrubbed
of machine paths (`$VENV`, `<dotagents-checkout>` placeholders) purely because
`--repo-hygiene` scans tracked files. That sanitization cost is gone now, and
plans here follow the same rule as every other project.

## Effect on [D55](D55.md)

D55's guard (refuse to link a `.agents` with tracked files) still stands and
still fires here — `.agents/dotagents/` is 58 tracked files. This repo remains
the one project that is never `dotagents link`ed. The move shrinks what would
be lost from 70 files to 58, but does not change the answer.

The alternative — moving the log too, so `.agents/` could be fully ignored and
the guard deleted — was rejected for the CHANGELOG reason above.
