A project's root `AGENTS.md` is its always-loaded core: always-on facts + routing list only. Topical detail → `<project>/.agents/{kb,flows,references}/`, loaded on match. Executors move overgrown notes into `.agents/kb/<topic>.md` leaving a routing line. `.agents/AGENTS.md` stays user-managed ([[D19]]).
