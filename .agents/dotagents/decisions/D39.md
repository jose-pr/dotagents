# D39: practical GPT-5.6 task routing

Date: 2026-07-16

## Decision

Use GPT-5.6 Luna high for planning and scoped implementation, Luna medium for
mechanical work, and Terra medium only when unresolved uncertainty spans multiple
subsystems. Use Sol low for independent review, medium after contradictory findings
or one failed targeted repair, and high only for a named critical boundary. Map
nominal Sol medium/high/xhigh to actual low/medium/high; `max` requires approval.

## Rationale

Field reports and observed Luna-high behavior favor a capable daily route without
default Sol spend. Higher Sol effort can expand scope, so escalate on failure,
cross-system uncertainty, blast radius, or recovery cost. This is a routing
heuristic, not a benchmark or universal ranking.
